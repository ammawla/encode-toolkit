name: track-experiments
description: Track ENCODE experiments locally with publications and provenance
---

Track ENCODE experiments in a local SQLite database with associated publications, pipeline info, and provenance chains.

Use `encode_track_experiment` to add experiments. Use `encode_list_tracked` to view your library. Use `encode_get_citations` to export citations as BibTeX or RIS.

Refer to the track-experiments skill for detailed guidance.
